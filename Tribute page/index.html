A . P . J Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Missile Man of India

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was an Indian scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu on October 15, 1931 and was popularly referred to as the "Missile Man of India" for his work on the development of India's missile technology. Before serving as President, he worked as a scientist and aerospace engineer at DRDO and ISRO. He was known for his simplicity and promoting education and was fondly called "People's President".
